Intersection of Quantum Learning Theory, Sensing, and Verification

27 April – 01 May 2026 | EPFL Campus

This workshop brings together the communities of quantum learning theory, quantum sensing, and quantum verification to explore their shared challenges and opportunities. While each field has made significant independent progress, interaction between them has historically been limited despite their closely related goals. Motivated by both rigorous theory and rapid experimental advances, the workshop aims to foster meaningful cross-disciplinary dialogue, with a particular focus on the capabilities of near- and intermediate-term quantum devices. Key themes include leveraging learning-theoretic and algorithmic techniques to enhance quantum sensing, developing improved noise models and error mitigation strategies, using insights from sensing and metrology to inspire new quantum algorithms, and addressing the critical challenge of verifying quantum computations on noisy devices that exceed classical simulation capabilities. By uniting leading researchers across domains, the workshop seeks to bridge theory and practice and catalyse new collaborative directions in quantum technologies.

Recommended Restaurants

Food in Lausanne is generally expensive (welcome to Switzerland) and under-whelming. But here are some less bad options as recommended by Zoe's group:

Brasserie de Montbenon

Allée E. Ansermet 3 , 1003 Lausanne

Location of the conference dinner.

Vieille Ouchy

Place du Port 3, 1006 Lausanne

If you need your cheese fix this does fondu in a cute old building by the lake.

Lacustre

Quai Jean-Pascal Delamuraz 1, 1006 Lausanne

Nice lake side location. Ok pizzas/bar food.

Umamido

Boulevard de grancy 46, 1006 lausanne

Unconventional (but good!) ramen.

L'Abordage

Avenue du Léman 67, 1025 St-Sulpice

Italian endorsed pizza/pasta near EPFL.

Shiraz

Av. du Léman 19, 1005 Lausanne

Great authentic Iranian food.

Reflets de Perse

Av. Emile-Henri-Jaques-Dalcroze 9, 1007 Lausanne

Inauthentic Iranian food (outstanding Iranian cocktails) with very scenic terrace views onto the lake.

[Fusian asian buffet by lake]

Very scenic terrace views onto lake. Fusion asian. Mixed reviews of food. All you can eat. Chaotic service.

Pré-Fleuri

Av. d'Ouchy 22, 1006 Lausanne

Georgian food in Sous-gare (bougy location between main station and lake).

My Sandwiches

Rue Saint-Martin 9, 1003 Lausanne

Cheap option in Lausanne, very tasty vietnamese sandwich, closes early around 6 PM

Restaurant la Jonque

Av. de Cour 15, 1007 Lausanne

Next to botanic garden, real Chinese restaurant, non-greasy Cantonese kitchen, and the only restaurant in Lausanne serving handmade dim sum.

Holy Cow

EPFL and Rue des Terreaux 10, 1003 Lausanne

The most highly debated food option in Lausanne. Viewed by some as a perfectly decent and convenient fast food burger joint at EPFL. Viewed by others as poor quality meat with legends of giving food poisoning. Opinions tend to correlate strongly with whether or not McDonald's was viewed as a treat growing up and whether or not they get excited by the thought of "in-and-out" burgers as they head west.
