### Sergi Masot Llima (Post Doc)

Sergi Masot Llima was raised in Barcelona, where he studied Physics and Mathematics at UB. He acquired his first practical experience both with quantum algorithms and Switzerland during his MSc at ETHZ. Afterwards, he pursued his PhD in the Barcelona Supercomputing Center, mixing quantum computing with HPC close to his home and loved ones. After graduating in 2026, he is now again at the foot of the Alps, working as a postdoc at QIC and enjoying the views of the Lac Léman. He's interested in understanding the role of quantum resources to discover better classical simulation techniques of quantum devices, but you often find him designing quantum algorithms and playing around with their numerics. Outside of work, he's as joyful indoors on a rainy day, reading or playing videogames, as much as he thrives with outdoor activities under the sun, including running, hiking and, perhaps unsurprisingly, climbing.
