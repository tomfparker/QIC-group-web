### Slimane Thabet (Post Doc)

Slimane is from Bordeaux, France, but traded vineyards on the Atlantic coast for the bustle of Paris, where he studied at École Polytechnique. He then moved across town to Sorbonne University for a PhD carried out in collaboration with the quantum computing company Pasqal. He has now crossed another border to join the QIC group at EPFL as a postdoc and is slowly coming to terms with the fact that Swiss cheese and wine might actually rival the French equivalents (on good days). In the lab, he enjoys writing optimized numerical code as much as proving analytical bounds, and is interested in quantum simulation, random circuits, and machine learning. Outside of work, he likes hiking, trying new foods and cooking techniques, and started winter sports.
